qdarts.noise_processes.OU_process¶

class qdarts.noise_processes.OU_process(sigma, tc, dt, num_elements)¶

Implements the Ornstein-Uhlenbeck noise process

This noise is independent of v. We have that

\[\epsilon(v)_{t+1}=a \epsilon(v)_t + \sqrt{1-a^2} \sigma \epsilon_t\]

Where \(\epsilon_t\) is independent gaussian noise and

\[a=e^{-\frac{\Delta t}{t_c}}\]

It is possible to generate multiple independent samples of the same process simultaneously.

__init__(sigma, tc, dt, num_elements)¶

Instantiates the OU process with its process parameters.

Parameters:

  • sigma (float) – Standard deviation of the OU process

  • tc (float) – time correlation parameters, the higher the more samples are correlated

  • dt (float) – time step between samples. The higher, the less samples are correlated

  • num_elements (int) – How many independnet samples are computed simultaneously
